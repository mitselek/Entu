L. lactis dairy starters

Rada L. lactis dairy starters
Short Name L. lactis dairy starters
Pealkiri Optimisation of media and growth conditions for the production of high yield biomass of Lactococcus lactis with high acidification activity
Kirjeldus Lactococcus lactis is and important organism in producing fermented dairy products. In order to produce an effective dairy starter it is important to understand and improve growth of L. lactis in milk. What is more, it is necessary to increase biomass yield and optimize the growth conditions of industrial starter production. All the latter issues are targeted in current project in co-operation with DuPont France.
